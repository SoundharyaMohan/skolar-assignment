1. Sitharamam
    • Year:2022
    • Hero:Dulquer Salman
    • Heroine:Mrunal Thakur

      • "Sita Ramam" is a poignant love story set against a backdrop of conflict and cross-cultural dynamics.

      Plot Summary:

      The film begins with Afreen (Rashmika Mandanna), a rebellious student in London, being tasked by her grandfather's will to deliver a long-lost letter to Sita Mahalakshmi (Mrunal Thakur). Reluctantly, Afreen travels to India and delves into the mystery of the letter, leading to the discovery of a beautiful yet tragic love story. The narrative then shifts to the 1960s and 70s, where Lieutenant Ram (Dulquer Salmaan), a soldier posted in Kashmir, receives a series of anonymous love letters from Sita Mahalakshmi. Intrigued and enamored by her words, Ram embarks on a journey to find Sita. As their paths cross, a deep and genuine love blossoms between them. However, their love story is fraught with challenges, including societal norms, political unrest, and personal sacrifices. The film weaves between the present and the past, revealing how Ram's and Sita's love faces trials and tribulations. Afreen's journey to deliver the letter becomes a voyage of self-discovery as she learns about love, duty, and the indomitable human spirit through Ram and Sita’s story.

  2. Majili
    • Year:2019
    • Hero:Naga Chaithanya
    • Heroine:Samantha Ruth Prabhu

      • Plot Summary

      The film opens with Poorna (Naga Chaitanya), a once-promising cricketer whose dreams are shattered due to personal and professional failures. His life is filled with bitterness and regret, largely because of his unfulfilled love for Anshu (Divyansha Kaushik), his childhood sweetheart. Poorna and Anshu's passionate relationship falls apart due to unforeseen circumstances, leading to a profound heartbreak for Poorna. After this tragic love affair, Poorna descends into a state of despair, unable to move past his failed dreams and lost love. In the midst of his emotional turmoil, Sravani (Samantha Ruth Prabhu), a kind and patient young woman, enters his life. Sravani has harbored feelings for Poorna since their youth and silently supports him despite his flaws and indifference towards her. Sravani marries Poorna, hoping to bring him out of his sorrow and help him find a purpose again. Their relationship starts on a rocky note, with Poorna still pining for Anshu and neglecting Sravani's affection. However, Sravani’s unwavering love and commitment gradually begin to melt Poorna’s hardened heart. As the story unfolds, Poorna begins to realize the depth of Sravani's love and her silent sacrifices for him. He confronts his past, acknowledges his mistakes, and embarks on a journey of personal growth. This transformative journey leads him to understand the true meaning of love and commitment. By the climax, Poorna recognizes Sravani's significance in his life, reconciling with his past and embracing his future with her. The film ends on a hopeful note, showcasing how love, patience, and understanding can heal even the deepest wounds.

    • Heroine:Tu Tontawan Tantivejaku

      • Plot Summary

      F4 Thailand: Boys Over Flowers" follows the journey of Gorya (Tu Tontawan Tantivejakul), an ordinary girl who navigates the challenges of attending a prestigious high school dominated by a powerful clique known as the F4. The Story: Introduction to Gorya and F4: Gorya is a bright and determined girl from a humble background who secures a scholarship to attend a prestigious private high school. At the school, she encounters the F4, a group of four elite boys led by Thyme (Bright Vachirawit Chivaaree), known for their wealth, power, and notoriety. The other members of F4 are Ren (Dew Jirawat Sutivanichsak), Kavin (Win Metawin Opas-iamkajorn), and MJ (Nani Hirunkit Changkham). Bullying and Resistance: The F4 rules the school with an iron fist, and Thyme, the leader, targets Gorya after she stands up to their bullying tactics. Despite the harassment, Gorya refuses to back down, earning the respect of some and the enmity of others. Love and Conflict: As Gorya continues to challenge Thyme's authority, he begins to develop unexpected feelings for her, which complicates his life and his relationship with his friends. Meanwhile, Gorya finds herself drawn to Ren, a sensitive and mysterious member of F4 who seems to understand her struggles. Transformation and Redemption: The series explores Gorya’s resilience and her influence on Thyme, who gradually transforms from a ruthless bully to a more empathetic and understanding individual. Gorya's interactions with the F4 lead to personal growth and a re-evaluation of what it means to have true power and influence. Conclusion: The story unfolds through a series of emotional and dramatic events, focusing on themes of love, redemption, friendship, and the courage to stand up for what is right. Gorya and Thyme's evolving relationship becomes central to the narrative, showcasing their journey from antagonists to partners.

  5. My Girlfriend Is An Alien
    • Year:2019
    • Hero:Bie Thassapak Hsu
    • Heroine:Wan Peng

      • Plot Summary

      Accidental Arrival: Chai Xiaoqi (Wan Peng), an alien from the "Cape Town Planet," finds herself stranded on Earth after a spacecraft malfunction. She is an adorable, naive, and bubbly alien who ends up in human form on Earth. Unique Abilities and Challenges: Chai Xiaoqi possesses unique abilities, such as mind-reading and manipulating weather, which she must conceal to blend in with humans. However, she faces significant challenges, including retaining her energy by recharging through a mysterious source and managing her disguise. Encounter with Fang Leng: Fang Leng (Bie Thassapak Hsu) is the CEO of a large company, characterized by his aloof and skeptical nature, largely due to a traumatic past that affects his ability to trust and love. He suffers from a rare condition causing him to forget any woman who has romantic feelings for him after it rains. Chai Xiaoqi's life takes a dramatic turn when she saves Fang Leng during a traffic accident, leading to a series of comic and romantic encounters. Fang Leng is unaware of her extraterrestrial origins and finds himself reluctantly drawn to her quirky and unpredictable behavior. Love and Secrets: Despite his initial coldness, Fang Leng gradually falls for Xiaoqi’s genuine and lively personality. Meanwhile, Xiaoqi struggles with her growing feelings for Fang Leng and the danger of revealing her true identity. As their relationship develops, Xiaoqi's secret identity as an alien and her mission to return to her home planet pose constant threats to their love. Conflicts and Resolutions: The series explores various conflicts, including Fang Leng’s unresolved issues with his family, Xiaoqi's efforts to fit into human society, and the interference from Fang Leng's jealous ex-girlfriend and other external factors. Xiaoqi’s alien background and her attempts to hide it create misunderstandings and complications in her relationship with Fang Leng, leading to moments of comedy, drama, and heartfelt romance. Conclusion: The series progresses towards Xiaoqi and Fang Leng confronting their feelings, overcoming obstacles, and finding a way to be together despite the differences in their worlds. Their journey emphasizes themes of love, acceptance, and the magic of finding connection beyond boundaries.
