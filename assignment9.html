About BTS

BTS, also known as the Bangtan Boys, is a South Korean boy band formed by Big Hit Entertainment. The group debuted in 2013 and has since become a global phenomenon, known for their influential music, outstanding choreography, and powerful fanbase known as ARMY.

Members

BTS consists of seven talented members:

  • RM (Leader, Rapper)
  • Jin (Vocalist)
  • Suga (Rapper)
  • J-Hope (Rapper, Dancer)
  • Jimin (Vocalist, Dancer)
  • V (Vocalist)
  • Jungkook (Vocalist, Dancer, Maknae)

Discography

BTS has an extensive discography, including:

  • Dark & Wild (2014)
  • Wings (2016)
  • Love Yourself: Tear (2018)
  • Map of the Soul: 7 (2020)
  • BE (2020)

Achievements

BTS has achieved numerous milestones, including:

  • First Korean group to top the Billboard 200.
  • Winner of multiple Billboard Music Awards.
  • Record-breaking social media presence and music video views.
  • Grammy nominations and performances.

Fun Facts

  • BTS means "Bulletproof Boy Scouts" in Korean.
  • They have their own mobile game called "BTS World."
  • BTS's fan base is called ARMY, which stands for "Adorable Representative M.C. for Youth."
  • Their music often addresses themes like mental health and self-love.
